About Boise

Boise is Idaho’s capital and the most populous city in the state, known for its riverfront parks, cycling paths along the Greenbelt, and trail access to the Boise Foothills. The nickname “City of Trees” reflects its extensive urban canopy and outdoor focus.

  • State: Idaho
  • Region: Treasure Valley
  • Elevation: ~2,700 ft (820 m)
  • Known for: Outdoor recreation, Basque culture, and a vibrant downtown
